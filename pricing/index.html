Pricing#

We charge based on your usage, which is defined by a Tier.

What is a Tier?

  • Each tier includes 250MB and 20 endpoints.
  • 0.10$ per day per tier.
  • The first tier is for free, forever!

To charge our users fairly, we check daily what is the service usage (endpoints + space) to find which tier is used.

Check the following examples:#

UsagePrice
200 MB and 10 endpointsTier 1 (free)
400 MB and 10 endpointsTier 2 (0.10$)
200 MB and 25 endpointsTier 2 (0.10$)
200 MB and 45 endpointsTier 3 (0.20$)
600 MB and 20 endpointsTier 3 (0.20$)
900 MB and 20 endpointsTier 4 (0.30$)

In the last example, Tier 4 means that you pay for Tier 2 + Tier 3 + Tier 4, 0.30$ in total.

info

Pricing is still under development, until it's completed you can use the service completely for free. After you can use our free Tier.
